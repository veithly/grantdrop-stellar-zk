# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are grant or reward claimants who want to receive a public Stellar-visible receipt without exposing the private fact that makes them eligible. Secondary users are campaign operators and public inspectors who need to verify accepted, rejected, and already-claimed states without seeing private inputs.

## Product Purpose

GrantDrop is a proof-gated grant receipt product. It turns a hidden eligibility condition into an accepted, rejected, or already-claimed Stellar-linked receipt. Success means a fresh claimant can choose a private input, sign a claim, see a real state change, reopen the receipt, and inspect the proof and Stellar path.

## Brand Personality

Clear, trustworthy, kinetic. The product should feel like a claim booth where a receipt flips into view, not like a compliance dashboard or a cryptography console.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble verifier dashboards, KYC portals, admin tables, generic shadcn card grids, purple/blue AI gradients, proof-log pages, or landing pages that delay the first action.

## Design Principles

1. Receipt first, proof second.
2. One visible action must create a visible state change.
3. The interface must make valid, invalid, and duplicate inputs feel intentionally different.
4. Every paid state needs a proof and Stellar inspection path.
5. Recovery states are product states, not apologies.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for text contrast, keyboard access, and focus states. Use text plus shape/icon cues for accepted, rejected, pending, failed, and already-claimed states. Respect reduced motion: the receipt flip and duplicate shake must have non-motion equivalents.
