# Judge Magnet: GrantDrop

## Personal/customer scar

People want the reward but do not want to publish the private reason they qualify. Grant programs also need to block duplicate claims without turning the first screen into a compliance form.

## Attention ladder

- 0-10s: judge sees one campaign, one private input choice, and the CTA `Claim grant`.
- 10-30s: judge changes valid/invalid/duplicate input and signs or uses the client signer.
- 30-60s: receipt flips to accepted, rejected, or already claimed.
- 60-90s: judge opens Inspect receipt and sees nullifier, proof/public inputs, and Stellar reference.

## Judge success metrics

- Judge can say the product in one sentence: "It pays eligible people without showing why they qualify."
- Judge can cause a state change in under 60 seconds.
- Judge can inspect proof and Stellar evidence without reading code first.
- Judge sees duplicate prevention as a product state, not an error.

## Ship cut

Keep one campaign, one proof rule, one nullifier, one wallet/client signer path, one receipt route, and one inspection path. Cut admin dashboards, real KYC, production grants, mainnet, analytics, and extra eligibility templates.
